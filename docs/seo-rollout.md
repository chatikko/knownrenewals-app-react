# SEO Rollout and Measurement Playbook

## Google Search Console Steps
1. Submit `https://knowrenewals.com/sitemap.xml` in Search Console.
2. Run URL Inspection for:
   - `https://knowrenewals.com/`
   - `https://knowrenewals.com/renewal-tracking-software`
   - `https://knowrenewals.com/blog/how-to-track-subscription-renewals`
   - `https://knowrenewals.com/blog/how-to-manage-saas-renewals`
   - `https://knowrenewals.com/blog/avoid-missing-renewal-deadlines`
3. Click **Request Indexing** for each inspected URL.
4. Re-check coverage and enhancement reports weekly after deployment.

## Primary KPI
- Qualified leads from organic traffic (trial/signup conversions attributed to organic sessions).

## Secondary KPIs
- Non-brand organic clicks and impressions on priority URLs.
- CTR trend for homepage, pillar page, and article pages.
- Indexed page count for all marketing URLs in sitemap.

## 30/60/90 Cadence
### Day 7
- Confirm sitemap fetched successfully.
- Confirm homepage + 3 priority pages are indexed.

### Day 30
- Review CTR and query-match quality.
- Refresh article intros/meta descriptions if CTR is weak.

### Day 60
- Expand internal links from high-impression pages to highest-converting pages.
- Update underperforming pages with clearer intent alignment.

### Day 90
- Compare qualified lead lift vs baseline.
- Decide next batch of long-tail pages and refresh schedule.
